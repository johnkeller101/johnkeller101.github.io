# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** {{ site.time | date: '%B %d, %Y' }}

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how our apps handle your data.

<br>

## Data Collection

Our apps collect and store data locally on your device, including:
- App preferences and settings
- Usage history and statistics

This data stays on your device unless you enable cloud sync.

<br>

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your data syncs across your devices using your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and stored securely by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

<br>

## Bluetooth & Hardware

Some apps use Bluetooth to connect to external devices. No Bluetooth data is transmitted to external servers.

<br>

## Analytics

Our apps do not collect analytics or tracking data.

<br>

## Third Parties

Our apps do not share any data with third parties.

<br>
