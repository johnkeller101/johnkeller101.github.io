# Changelog

Keep track of all the updates and improvements to CuffMate.

<br>

### `Latest`
# **Version 1.0**
Initial release of CuffMate - your companion app for SAGA BFR cuffs.

#### Features
- Bluetooth device discovery and connection
- Limb occlusion pressure (LOP) calibration
- Timed and set-based workouts
- Live Activity support (Lock Screen & Dynamic Island)
- Workout history tracking
- iCloud sync across devices
- Support for 34 languages
